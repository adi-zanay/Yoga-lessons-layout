Open Classes


Ready to wind-down and leave your workday behind? My Hatha yoga classes are built on a supportive atmosphere that offers space to explore your individual wellbeing through a mindful flow. All levels of yoga experience and fitness are welcome.






Private Sessions


Treat yourself with individual attention to expand your yoga experience. Private yoga sessions are great for individuals or small groups who would like to focus on more specific goals for their practice.






Office Yoga


As a former office yogi, I understand the challenges of keeping work and life in balance. Yoga and meditation are proven ways to manage stress and prevent burnout – Help create a healthy and sustainable work environment for your employees and profit from tax deductions.






Mindfulness Workshop


Guided mindfulness is scientifically proven to be an effective tool for reducing stress and promoting a healthy lifestyle. If you are interested in exploring ways to cultivate a sustainably productive work culture, mindfulness workshops are a good place to start.
